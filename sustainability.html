Our Sustainability
Commitment.

How We Operate

Doing the right thing
quietly.

The Blonde Hedgehog has been committed to sustainable, environmentally responsible operation from the start. We work with the Alderney Wildlife Trust and operate in as environmentally sound a manner as possible.

We believe that true luxury and genuine sustainability are entirely compatible. Our approach is practical and ongoing — not performative.

Our Commitments

  • Plastic-free throughout — no single-use plastics on the property
  • Plum & Ashby and Damana sustainable bath products throughout
  • Belu water (profits go to WaterAid) throughout the hotel
  • LED lighting and boilers on timers throughout
  • Water-saving devices and water butts on garden pipes
  • Energy-efficient EcoKnit robes and towels in all rooms
  • Full recycling programme throughout the property
  • Extra insulation added during refurbishment to save energy
  • Partners with Alderney Wildlife Trust
The Kitchen Garden

Growing our own,
composting the rest.

Our kitchen garden supplies vegetables, herbs and edible flowers to the restaurant. Vegetable waste and coffee grounds go directly into the compost. Almost all other ingredients are sourced on-island — supporting local farmers, fishermen and producers, and keeping food miles as short as possible.

Alderney Wildlife Trust

Protecting what
makes this place special.

We work closely with the Alderney Wildlife Trust to help protect the island's extraordinary natural environment — the gannet colonies, the blonde hedgehogs, the puffins and storm petrels. Ask our team about Wildlife Trust guided tours during your stay.
